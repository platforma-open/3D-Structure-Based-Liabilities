First sequence-based liability detection for therapeutic antibody candidates:
unpaired cysteines, deamidation hotspots (N-G, N-S), N-linked glycosylation
sequons (N-X-[S/T]), and oxidation-prone residues (M, W). Insertion-code aware
(handles antibody-numbering positions like 100A, 100B in CDRs). Removes the
unused descriptive PDB visualizations and the original Hello World scaffolding.
